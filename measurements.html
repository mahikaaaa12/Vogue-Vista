Methodology

Proportion
ratios decoded

Our classification system uses scientifically-validated proportional thresholds between four key skeletal landmarks — shoulders, chest, waist and hips — to determine body shape with precision. No camera required; just four measurements.

01
Enter Measurements

Input shoulder width, chest/bust, waist, and hip circumference in centimetres or inches. All values are instantly converted for consistency.

02
Ratio Analysis

The system calculates hip-to-waist ratio, shoulder-to-hip differential, and chest-to-waist differential to map your proportional signature.

03
Shape Classification

Measurements are evaluated against gender-specific thresholds to produce an accurate classification from five female and five male archetypes.

Classification Reference

Body Shape Archetypes

01 — Pear

Hips wider than shoulders, defined waist

02 — Hourglass

Equal bust and hips, narrow waist

03 — Inv. Triangle

Shoulders broader than hips

04 — Rectangle

Shoulders, waist and hips aligned

05 — Apple

Weight at waist, high hip-waist ratio

01 — Trapezoid

Broad shoulders, chest wider than waist

02 — Triangle

Waist wider than shoulders and chest

03 — Oval

Waist larger than chest and shoulders

04 — Rectangle

Shoulders, waist and chest uniformly aligned

05 — Athletic / V

Shoulders > chest > waist, V-taper
