Behind the Lens

A photograph captures what a tape measure cannot — the complete picture. Our model identifies your key skeletal reference points in a single pass, translating light and shadow into a precise morphological signature without you lifting a finger.

01
Choose Your Photo

Select a recent full-body image — ideally lit, front-facing, with arms slightly away from the torso. Fitted clothing helps the model read proportions more clearly. No studio required.

02
Vision Engine Maps Your Form

Our AI locates the four key anatomical landmarks — shoulder line, chest, natural waist and hip crest — then calculates the differential ratios that define your unique silhouette profile.

03
Receive a Precise Classification

Your silhouette is matched to the closest archetype from our library of ten body shape categories. You receive the classification name plus an explanation of the specific proportional cues that determined your result.
