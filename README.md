# ⚜️ Vogue Vista — AI Personal Stylist ⚜️

Discover Your True Palette. Master Your Silhouette.

Vogue Vista is a high-end AI styling platform that merges Color Intelligence with Silhouette Intelligence. It doesn't just tell you what looks good; it explains your unique morphological and tonal profile using advanced computer vision and data mapping.

🌟 Core Features

🎨 Color Intelligence (Skin Tone Analysis)

Discover which shades make you glow and which to avoid.

📸 Vision Analysis: AI samples skin pixels to calculate the undertone.

✍️ Diagnostic Quiz: A 5-step manual check for jewelry, vein, and sun-reaction traits.

✨ Result: A curated "Power Palette" with specific HEX codes for your wardrobe.

📏 Silhouette Intelligence (Body Analysis)

Understand your physique through the lens of morphological science.

🔳 Silhouette Scanning: Interactive UI that simulates a 3D scan of your body.

📐 Precise Metrics: Maps 40+ data points based on shoulder, bust, waist, and hip ratios.

👗 Shaping Advice: Provides tailored clothing cuts for Hourglass, Pear, Athletic, and more.
